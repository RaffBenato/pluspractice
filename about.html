About

I built this web application when my son was working towards his Eleven Plus Exams and it has been essential in his academic success. I decided to release as a public web site so that other kids can benefit from it. Although in his case it was used as a studying tool for the exams specifically, I have modified the application so that it doesn't have to be used for that particular objective. I believe that any child of primary school age can benefit from using this website to achieve academic improvements. The website is purposely built without the usual gimmicky colours, sounds and characters as it is my belief that children benefit from separating playing time from working time. With the usual apps/website developed for kids with the idea of "learning through play", the child is left to spend hours with a device. The though is that the child will learn as they kill time with the games but in reality the child will normally get distracted by the richness of the digital experience and not focus on the work. Children will benefit from short, focused work time. It doesn't have to take much of their time, 10 minutes daily should be enough to see real improvement in their academic abilities. If your goal is to prepare your child for the Eleven Plus Exams then I recommend using this application on top of some of the books we recommend in our Materials section.
