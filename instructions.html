Instructions

The way I advise you to use this web application is by asking the child to practice between 5 and 10 minutes daily. The app is made so that is better viewed and used on a mobile device. This is not supposed to be viewed by the child as a game or a way to spend time but as a focused activity to support their learning, just like homework would be. You can agree a target for each activity. For example you may ask them to work on their addition activity until they can get a result of 20 correct answers without any mistakes. They should work towards that target every, if the activity has levels the child can then move on to the next level once they have hit the agreed target. This is up to you and your child but you should choose a challenging level for every session. These sessions should be short but intense. There are no specific order in which to use the application.
